What are you waiting for? we have multiple experiences so you can have the best time with nature and friends!

Three types of experiences!

Family trip its the more relaxed and calm experience, where you and can enjoy the beauty of the nature, see the animal around you, where you can take a break from the hectic daily life. this experience is the best for families with small childs.

Clasic rafting is an experience where you and a group will go thoug the power of the river perfect midle point whe you will feel adrenaline and be able to admire the nature at the same time!

Rapids is an intense experience where you are going to ride the fastet seccions of the river where you and other 3 friend will experience the power of the water and have some funny moments. Not available for children under 13 years old.
